About Me

Who am i?

I'm Mailen Kleinman

I live in Buenos Aires, Argentina. Currently finishing my degree in Industrial Design at the University of Buenos Aires (UBA). I have several years of experience working in design. The last two years I got into the world of software and programming.

I provide design solutions for your project or digital product. I am also dedicated to organization and planning.
